<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10.5pt;">Your preferred name will not appear on your Student ID Card, only your given names will appear. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10.5pt;">Your preferred name should show in most places after a few days of being recorded in CQUniversity systems, including in Moodle and your email address, but some systems will still show your legal name.</span></p>
